About the Band

Sugar Jack was formed in 2005 by singer/guitarist Jon Mickey and bassist Eric Plantholt. The band was formed with the intention of playing music, both covers and originals, that both band members and audiences could feel connected to. Tragically, Jon Mickey was lost in a car accident in 2014. Eric and drummer Tony Bruno wanted to continue the band in Jon's memory. Tony DiLorenzo rejoined the band, Keith Lee was added as the lead singer and Matt Bressler was added to the band as a guitarist to round out the group.

Eric "erock" Plantholt - Bass, Vocals

Eric is an original founding member of Sugar Jack with good friend Jon Mickey. Has roots in alt metal, rock and funk rock. Eric started playing when he was 19 years old and has a strong passion for playing, writing and recording original music. Favorite bands and major influences include Red Hot Chili Peppers and Metallica.

Keith Lee - Lead Vocals

Keith joined Sugar Jack to form it's most recent lineup in 2016. Keith has always loved to sing and enjoyed performing for audiences. He loves 90s Alt. Rock and Classic Rock. His musical influences include Bob Marley, Sublime and Pearl Jam.

Tony "DiLo" DiLorenzo - Guitar

DiLo has been performing in the Baltimore music scene for over 30 years, most notably as lead guitarist for The Loft. His accomplishments with The Loft include opening for The Black Crowes in Ocean City, headlining college nights weekly at the original HammerJacks in Baltimore as well as performing on Star Search in 1995. His favorite musicians and biggest influences include ACDC, Manic Street Preachers, Joe Satriani, It Bites and Super Furry Animals.

Tony Bruno - Drums

Tony joined Sugar Jack in 2014 as their drummer and he loves to play live music and perform for audiences. His biggest influences as drummers are Phil Rudd of ACDC, John Bonham of Led Zepplin and Alex Van Halen of Van Halen. Tony's favorite bands include KISS, Van Halen and Aerosmith.

Matt Bressler - Guitar

Matt joined his first band, Sugar Jack, in 2016 to share lead and rhythm guitar duties. He loves to spend as much of his spare time as possible playing and praciticing guitar as well as writing music. His favorite guitarists and biggest influences are Jimi Hendrix, Tom Morello of Rage Against the Machine and John Frusciante of Red Hot Chili Peppers.
